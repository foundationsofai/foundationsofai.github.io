Foundations of Artificial Intelligence Seminar Series

by Foundations of Artificial Intelligence @ Georgia Institute of Technology

Welcome to the Student-Led AI Seminar Series, a seminar showcasing the latest research and developments in Artificial Intelligence. Our goal is to bring together students, postdocs, professors, and industry researchers to discuss a wide range of AI topics, including Machine Learning, Symbolic AI, AI Theory, AI Systems, and the intersection of AI and Programming Languages and Software Engineering (PLSE).
